- Cannabis cultivators and producers looking to streamline operations.
- Dispensaries seeking to improve inventory management and compliance.
- Cannabis distributors needing efficient task management and data integration.
- ERP system vendors interested in partnerships for integration solutions.

- Automated data entry reduces manual errors, saving time and increasing accuracy.
- Seamless integration with existing ERP systems enhances workflow efficiency.
- Task management features tailored specifically for cannabis industry operations.
- Compliance tracking to meet regulatory requirements, reducing legal risks.

- Direct sales through the company website and sales team.
- Partnerships with ERP vendors for co-marketing opportunities.
