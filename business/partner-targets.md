### Prioritization Criteria
1. **Affiliate/Revenue-Share Potential**: Target integrations that offer revenue-sharing models to create additional income streams.
2. **Market Relevance**: Focus on platforms that are widely adopted in the cannabis industry to ensure user familiarity and ease of adoption.
3. **Integration Effort**: Balance the integration effort with the potential value-add to ensure efficient resource allocation.

### Conclusion
The integration roadmap focuses on platforms that not only solve specific user jobs but also align with the cannabis industry's unique needs. Prioritizing partnerships with revenue-sharing opportunities will enhance the business model of cannabis-ops-sync while providing significant value to users.
