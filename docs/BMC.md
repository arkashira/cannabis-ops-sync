# Business Model Canvas - cannabis-ops-sync

## Customer Segments
- **Cultivation Facilities**: Large-scale cannabis growers with complex inventory and compliance needs
- **Dispensaries**: Retail locations requiring inventory tracking and sales reporting integration
- **Manufacturing Operations**: Facilities processing cannabis into various products with batch tracking requirements
- **Multi-State Operators (MSOs)**: Businesses operating across multiple jurisdictions with varying compliance requirements
- **Compliance Officers**: Professionals responsible for regulatory adherence and reporting
- **Small-to-Medium Cannabis Businesses**: Operators looking to streamline operations without enterprise-level resources

## Value Propositions
- **Automated Data Entry**: Reduce manual data entry by up to 80%, minimizing human error and saving valuable time
- **ERP Integration**: Seamless connectivity with major cannabis ERPs (MJ Freeway, BioTrack THC, Flowhub, etc.)
- **Compliance Automation**: Built-in regulatory compliance tracking for state and local cannabis regulations
- **Workflow Streamlining**: Customizable task management tailored to cannabis industry operations
- **Real-time Synchronization**: Instant data updates across cultivation, processing, and retail operations
- **Inventory Management**: Automated tracking from seed to sale with full traceability
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Comprehensive reporting for business intelligence and compliance documentation

## Channels
- **Direct Sales**: Through axentx website and sales team targeting cannabis operators
- **Industry Partnerships**: Co-marketing with ERP providers and cannabis industry consultants
- **Trade Shows**: Presence at cannabis industry conferences and exhibitions
- **Digital Marketing**: Targeted campaigns through cannabis industry publications and online platforms
- **Referral Program**: Incentivized referrals from existing customers and industry partners
- **Free Trial**: Limited-time demo version for evaluation
- **Industry Events**: Sponsorship and participation in cannabis business and technology events

## Customer Relationships
- **Automated Onboarding**: Self-service setup with guided tutorials and templates
- **Tiered Support**: Basic email support for all customers, dedicated account managers for enterprise clients
- **Training Resources**: Webinars, video tutorials, and comprehensive documentation
- **Community Forum**: Peer-to-peer support and best practice sharing
- **Regular Check-ins**: Proactive account management for enterprise clients
- **Feedback Integration**: Continuous product improvement based on customer input
- **Compliance Updates**: Automatic regulatory changes communicated to customers

## Revenue Streams
- **SaaS Subscriptions**: Tiered pricing based on facility size and transaction volume
  - Basic: $299/month for small operations
  - Professional: $799/month for medium facilities
  - Enterprise: Custom pricing for large MSOs
- **Implementation Fees**: One-time setup and integration costs ($1,500-$5,000)
- **Premium Support**: 24/7 support and dedicated account management ($200-$500/month)
- **Custom Development**: Tailored solutions for specific compliance needs (hourly or project-based)
- **API Access**: Fees for third-party developers integrating with the platform
- **Compliance Updates**: Annual subscription for regulatory change monitoring ($500-$2,000/year)
