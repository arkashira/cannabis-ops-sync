# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for cannabis-ops-sync

## Problem Statement

The cannabis industry is heavily regulated, and operators face significant challenges in managing their day-to-day operations, including tracking inventory, managing orders, and complying with regulatory requirements. Manual data entry and task management processes are prone to errors, leading to inefficiencies, compliance risks, and lost revenue opportunities.

## Target Users

* Cannabis industry operators (growers, processors, dispensaries, etc.)
* ERP system administrators and users
* Compliance and regulatory professionals

## Goals

* Automate data entry and task management processes for cannabis industry operators
* Integrate with ERP systems to streamline workflows and reduce manual errors
* Improve compliance and regulatory adherence
* Enhance operational efficiency and reduce costs
* Provide real-time visibility and insights into business operations

## Key Features (Prioritized)

### Must-Haves

1. **Automated Data Entry**: Integrate with ERP systems to automatically import and update data, reducing manual entry errors and increasing data accuracy.
2. **Task Management**: Provide a centralized task management system for cannabis industry operators to assign, track, and complete tasks related to inventory management, orders, and compliance.
3. **Compliance and Regulatory Tools**: Offer features to help operators comply with regulatory requirements, including tracking and reporting on inventory, waste, and other critical metrics.
4. **Real-time Visibility**: Provide real-time visibility into business operations, including inventory levels, orders, and task completion status.

### Nice-to-Haves

1. **Customizable Workflows**: Allow operators to create custom workflows and business rules to fit their specific needs.
2. **Integration with Additional Systems**: Integrate with other systems, such as CRM, accounting, and point-of-sale systems.
3. **Reporting and Analytics**: Provide advanced reporting and analytics capabilities to help operators make data-driven decisions.
4. **Mobile Access**: Offer mobile access to the platform for operators to manage their tasks and data on-the-go.

## Success Metrics

* Adoption rate of automated data entry and task management features
* Reduction in manual errors and compliance risks
* Increase in operational efficiency and reduction in costs
* User satisfaction and Net Promoter Score (NPS)
* Revenue growth and customer acquisition

## Scope

The scope of this project includes:

* Development of the cannabis-ops-sync platform
* Integration with ERP systems
* Development of automated data entry and task management features
* Compliance and regulatory tools
* Real-time visibility and insights
* Customizable workflows and business rules
* Integration with additional systems (optional)
* Reporting and analytics capabilities (optional)
* Mobile access (optional)

## Out-of-Scope

The following features and functionalities are out-of-scope for this project:

* Development of custom ERP systems or integrations with non-standard systems
* Development of custom workflows and business rules beyond the scope of the platform
* Integration with systems that require significant custom development or API development
* Development of advanced reporting and analytics capabilities beyond the scope of the platform
* Development of mobile apps beyond the scope of the platform
