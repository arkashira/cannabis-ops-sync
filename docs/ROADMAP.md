# ROADMAP.md

## Overview

The cannabis-ops-sync project aims to provide an automated data entry and task management platform for cannabis industry operators, integrating with ERP systems to streamline workflows and reduce manual errors. This roadmap outlines the key milestones and features for the project, with a focus on delivering a high-quality product that meets the needs of our target market.
